Mowgli

Taken from his village by Shere Khan the tiger, he escapes and runs into a cave where a wolf family live. Mowgli is a good, strong leader who honors the Jungle Law and respects the jungle traditions. He is eager to fight and defend his pack whenever he feels they are threatened.

Age: 10 years

Friends: Baloo, the bear, and Bagheera, the black panther.
